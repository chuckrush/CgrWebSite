Night Stand

Another table rescued from the side of the road while walking around the neighborhood. It was in pretty rough shape but met our need for a nightstand for the guest room. We cleaned it up and put it in to service.

Its junkiness always bothered me. I like nice things. The wife and I have always admired McKenzie Child furniture. They have really nice stuff but are not easy on the wallet. We decided to take a shot at painting the night stand in the McKenzie Child Style. This page will document that effort. As of April, 2021 I still have not started this project.
