Things I Like

I put a lot of stock in personal recommendations for products and services. Below are a few items I have had good experiences using. I have no commercial interest in these products and I have not received anything in return for these reviews.

Laptop Hood

Laptops are great. Computing power you can take anywhere.

Sunny days are great. Nothing like being outside under a blue sky.

Sunny days and laptops together? Not so great. It is tough to see the screen.

A laptop hood is a good solution. I bought a Think Tank Pixel Sunscreen V2.0 from amazon.com. It has worked well for me. My dad and I used it on our recent R&D Expedition, October, 2020.

I have one piece of advice when using a laptop hood. Be mindful of wind. When I worked at SRC I was out in the parking lot doing testing on code I had written to control and use an inertial navigation unit. It was a nice, sunny warm spring day and I was using a laptop hood. It was also a little windy. While I was away from the PC there was a gust of wind followed by the sound of a laptop and the parking lot getting better acquainted. You have been warned.

Laptop Stand

Laptops are great. Computing power you can take anywhere.

Laptop stands are great, a convenient place to use the laptop.

I bought this Quiklok Music Stand (LPH003) from Amazon.com a while back and have been pleased. I use it when I do radio stuff outside.

When not doing radio stuff, the laptop stand lives down in my wife's basement exercise area. She uses it control the computer in her workout area.
