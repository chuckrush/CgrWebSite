R & D Expedition, October, 2020

I think we all agree that Covid 19 sure is getting old. My wife and I made a decision to get out of White Plains and go on a vacation the second week of October, 2020. She picked out a great place, Opussum Creek Retreat, near Fayetteville, West Virginia. This is where the New River Gorge bridge is. If you re going that way, I give an unqualified 55 star recommendation for Oposum Creek Retreat. We rented a 2 bedroom cabion. The place was spotless, well provisioned, had a screened in porch with a hot tub, a grill, and a fire pit. Fantastic.

We asked my father to accompany us. He is a pretty chill guy. When I saw where we were going and my dad, an electricla engineer, said he would go with us I immediately thought "I am going to take my radio stuff and we can have some fun away from noisy White Plains, New York.""

I am fortunate to have a pretty cool wife who agreed this was a good idea, as long as I made time for her too. So, what we ended up doing was the wife and I went hiking in the mornings and my father and I played radio for 3 of the afternoons.
